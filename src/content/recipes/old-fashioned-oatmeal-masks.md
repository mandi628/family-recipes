# Old-Fashioned Oatmeal Masks

### For Oily Skin
* 1/2 cup cooked oatmeal
* 1 egg white
* 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
* 1/2 cup mashed apple

### For Dry Skin
* 1/2 cup cooked oatmeal
* 1 egg yolk
* 1/2 banana, mashed
* 1 tbsp honey

### For Normal Skin
* 1/2 cup cooked oatmeal
* 1 whole egg
* 1 tbsp almond oil

# Mix all the ingredients together into a smooth paste.
# Spread on your face and leave on for 15 minutes. Rinse your face clean with tepid water and pat dry.
