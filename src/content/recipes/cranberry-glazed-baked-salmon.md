# Cranberry-Glazed Baked Salmon

Makes 4 servings

* 1 C whole berry cranberry sauce
* 1/4 C honey
* 1/4 C soy sauce
* 2 cloves minced garlic
* 1 T minced fresh ginger
* 1/4 tsp pepper
* 4 skinless salmon filets (or 1-1/2 lbs)

# Combine glaze ingredients.
# Place salmon on greased baking sheet. Top with glaze.
# Bake at 350 until done (about 10-15 minutes).
