# Oma's Tortellini Surimi Salad

## Ingredients
* 2 10 oz. packages tortellini - choose your favorite type
* 1 pkg surimi chunks
* 1 jar artichoke hearts
* 1 jar/can hearts of palm
* 1 small jar pimentos, drained
* 1 can sweet corn or baby corn
* 1 cucumber, diced
* 1 pkg grape or cherry tomatoes, halved/quartered
* 1 large carrot, shredded
* 3 oz. crumbled feta
* 1/2 C mayonnaise
* 1/2 C sour cream
* 2 Tbsp lemon juice
* dillweed
* salt and pepper to taste

## Directions
# Cook tortellini, following package directions. Drain and set aside to cool.
# Lightly chop surimi chunks (halves should be good). Add on top of tortellini.
# Drain artichoke hearts and hearts of palm and chop lightly. Add on top of surimi.
# Drain pimentos, drain corn, dice cucumber, prep tomatoes, shred carrot. Add to the bowl.
# Sprinkle crumbled feta over the salad.
# In a separate bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, lemon juice, dillweed, and salt and pepper. Stir until combined. Taste and adjust as needed.
# Pour dressing over salad and stir until evenly coated. Refrigerate salad for at least one hour before serving.
