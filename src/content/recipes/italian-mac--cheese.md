# Italian Mac & Cheese

## Ingredients
* 1 recipe [Alfredo Sauce](Alfredo Sauce.html), or [Alfredo Sauce on a Budget](Alfredo Sauce on a Budget.html)
* 1 pkg of your favorite pasta
* Shredded mozzarella, Parmesan, or provolone cheeses
** You can also use slices, if that's easier to get

### Add-ins
* broccoli florets
* Italian sausage
* shredded chicken
* shrimp
* fresh diced tomatoes
* peas
* bacon, cooked and chopped

## Directions
# Prepare the sauce.
# Cook the noodles.
# In your favorite casserole dish, stir together the noodles and other add-ins you want.
# Top with the sauce and stir together well.
# Finish it off with whatever cheese you have on hand.
# Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes, until bubbly and the cheese has melted and is starting to brown.
