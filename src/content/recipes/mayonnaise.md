!Mayonnaise

* 1-1/4 cups vegetable oil
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 egg
* 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

# In a blender, mix 1/4 cup oil, salt, and egg.
# With the blender on, slowly pour another 1/4 cup oil, in a very thin stream, into the whirling mixture. Keep the blender mixing.
# Slowly pour into the whirling mixture half of the vinegar in a slow, steady stream. Keep the blender mixing.
# Slowly pour into the mixture another 1/4 cup of oil followed by the rest of the vinegar. Continue blending.
# Slowly add the rest of the oil. You should now have a thick, white, creamy mayonnaise.
# Pour your mayonnaise into a clean container and store in the refrigerator. You can use this recipe in any recipe that calls for mayonnaise.
