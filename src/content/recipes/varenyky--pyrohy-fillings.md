# Varenyky & Pyrohy Fillings

Use these with [Varenyky](Varenyky.html) or [Pyrohy](Pyrohy.html).

## Meat & Rice Filling

<<<
This is one of our favorite fillings. Give it a try and make it your own!
<<<

* brown gravy packet
* browned ground beef
* cooked rice
* frozen peas

# Make up the gravy packet, following the directions.
# Add ground beef, rice, and peas until everything is coated in gravy.
# Cool before using.

## Potato Filling

* 1-2 cups mashed potatoes (instant is fine)
* 1/4 - 1/2 cup sharp cheese (Cheddar, farmer's cheese, etc.)

# Prepare the potatoes as you like. Don't add milk, because you want them on the dry side.
# Stir in your preferred cheese. Be careful not to add too much, because the potatoes will get gluey.
# Cool before using.

## Sauerkraut Filling

* 1 cup sauerkraut
* bacon bits
* salt & pepper
* sour cream

# Rinse & drain the sauerkraut. If the pieces are on the long side, chop them lightly with a knife.
# Stir in the bacon bits, salt & pepper, and enough sour cream to moisten everything and keep it together.
