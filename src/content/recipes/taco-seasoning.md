# Taco Seasoning

* 3 T chili powder
* 1 T garlic powder
* 1 T onion powder
* 1 T ground cumin
* 1 T smoked paprika
* 1 T chipotle powder
* 1 T dried oregano
* 2 tsp ground coriander
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp citric acid (optional)

Combine everything in a bowl, season to taste and store for tacos, burritos, whatever you like!

Use 3-4 T per pound of "meat".
