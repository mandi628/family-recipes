# Flea Repellent for Cats

## Ingredients

* 1 C apple cider vinegar
* 1 C water
* 10 drops Neem oil
* 10 drops peppermint oil

## Directions

# Combine all ingredients in a spray bottle and shake well.
# Shake well before each use.
# Lightly spray on cat's fur or wipe on with a clean cloth.
# Or, spray a flea comb and comb through the cat's fur.
# You can re-apply every 24-48 hours.
# You can also use this on household surfaces where fleas like to hide - carpets, rugs, sofas, bedding, etc.
