# Easy Pan-Cooked Fish Filets

### Ingredients
* fish filets - any type you prefer, and at least one per person
* seasonings
* vegetable oil

### Steps
# Thaw the fish filets, if they are frozen. Remove from packaging and pat dry.
# Preheat a frying pan and add vegetable oil.
# Season filets as preferred and place in the frying pan. Here are some suggestions:
## [Taco Seasoning](Taco Seasoning.html)
## Lemon pepper seasoning
## Lemon juice and dill
## Adobo seasoning
## Season salt
# Cover the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes.
# Flip the filets, cover and cook for 2-3 minutes more.
# Top with anything you like (fresh herbs, bread crumbs, salsa, pizza sauce, etc.) and serve.
