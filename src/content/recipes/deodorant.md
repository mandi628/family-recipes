!Deodorant

* 1/2 C baking soda (may reduce to as little as 1/4 C for more sensitive skin)
* 1/2 C arrowroot powder^^1^^ or cornstarch
* 5 T coconut oil, melted or softened
* 20 drops essential oil (tea tree oil, lavender, rosemary, etc.)

# Melt the coconut oil in a double boiler.
# Add the baking soda and arrowroot powder and mix well.
# Stir in desired essential oils.
# Pour into a clean, airtight container and store at room temperature.

Use your finger or a popsicle stick to apply.

For a firmer "stick form" deodorant, add the following:

* 1 T beeswax
* 1 T Shea butter

#Pour into reusable deodorant containers^^2^^ and refrigerate until solid.

^^1^^ Can be purchased at most grocery stores.
