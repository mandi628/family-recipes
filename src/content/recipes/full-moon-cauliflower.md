# Full Moon Cauliflower

Makes 4 servings

* 1/4 C butter
* 2 T flour
* 1 C milk
* 3 eggs
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1/4 C bread crumbs
* 1 C grated Parmesan chese
* 1 C grated white cheese
* 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets and cooked

Source: Telesco, pg 292
