# Homemade Cream Cheese

!!Ingredients
* 1 recipe [Farmer's Cheese](Farmer's Cheese.html)
* 2-4 T milk or heavy cream
* Salt, to taste
* 1 tsp lemon juice, optional

## Directions
# After making the [Farmer's Cheese](Farmer's Cheese.html) and letting it drain for about 30 minutes, add it and 2T of the milk or heavy cream to a food processor or blender.
# Blend until smooth. If a softer consistency is desired, add more milk or heavy cream.
# If a tangy taste is desired, add the lemon juice and blend again.
# Stir in the salt and place in a container for storage.
