# Basic Cheese Sauce

This sauce is great for Pretzels, Nachoes, grits, etc.

## Ingredients
* 1 recipes [White Sauce](White Sauce.html)
* 1 C shredded cheese
* seasoning

## Directions
# Prepare the [White Sauce](White Sauce.html)
# Slowly stir in the cheese, a little at a time, adding more once the first addition is well combined.
# Add your chosen seasoning and stir well. Serve immediately, or store for later.

## Add-ins
* For Pretzels, you can start with Sharp Cheddar cheese, and add a little ground mustard at the seasoning stage, and definitely some pepper.
* For Nachoes, use Cheddar cheese and add hot sauce, or [taco seasoning](Taco Seasoning.html) for a little heat.
* For an Italian flavor profile, use Mozarella and/or Parmesan, and season with Italian seasoning, or just some oregano.
