# Red Kidney Bean Salad

2 servings; 7 minutes

## Ingredients

!!!Salad
* 1 can kidney beans, drained & rinsed
* 1/2 can sweet corn, drained
* 1/2 cucumber, finely diced
* 1/2 C parsley, fresh
* 1 spring onion
* 3 oz. feta cheese, crumbled

### Dressing
* 1/2 lime, juiced
* 2 tsp olive oil
* 1 tsp mustard (Dijon, or English)
* 1 tsp cumin, ground
* 1/2 tsp oregano, dried
* 1 tsp honey
* salt and pepper to taste

## Directions
# Stir together kidney beans, sweet corn, cumber, parsley, and spring onion.
# Add the crumbled feta and stir.
# In a separate bowl, combine the dressing ingredients and stir until combined.
# Pour dressing over salad and stir. Serve immediately.

### Make Ahead
Divide into two containers and store in refrigerator for up to 3 days.
