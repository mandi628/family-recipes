# Mexican Meatball Soup
Cook time: 4 hours in crockpot, 1 hour on stovetop

* 1 lb frozen Italian meatballs
* 2/3 cup onion (one whole onion, chopped)
* 2/3 cup green bell pepper (one whole pepper, chopped)
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 32 oz. beef broth (2 cans or one box)
* 16-24 oz. jar salsa (choose your own heat)
* 1 Tbsp chili powder
* 1/4 tsp ground cumin
* 1 cup pasta shells or elbow macaroni
* 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
* 1/4 cup sour cream
* 1 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

# Saute onion and green pepper until onions are softened. Place in crockpot with meatballs, garlic, beef broth, salsa, chili powder, and cumin. Stir to combine.
# Cover and cook on low 5-7 hours (or high 2-1/2 to 3-1/2 hours).
# Add pasta, stir well, cover, and cook on low an additional hour (30 min on high), or until pasta is tender.
# Garnish with cheese, sour cream, and cilantro.
------
## Stovetop Instructions

# In a stock pot, saute onions and green peppers.
# Add meatballs, garlic, beef broth, salsa, and seasonings. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered, on medium-low for 20 minutes.
# Add pasta, then recover and simmer an additional 20 minutes, until pasta is cooked.
# Serve with cheese, sour cream, and cilantro.
