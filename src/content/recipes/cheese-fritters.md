# Cheese Fritters
(Russian)  

Makes 12 servings.

* 1 C flour
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 3 tsp sugar
* 5 egg yolks, beaten
* 6 C crumbled [farmer's cheese](Farmer's Cheese.html)
* Sour cream & chives

# Mix the flour, salt, and sugar in a medium-sized bowl.
# Stir in the egg yolks and add the cheese, mixing until sticky.
# Form dough into two logs, 2-3 inches thick. If the mixture is too dry to form, add a little water; if too wet, add a little flour.
# Chill for 30 minutes.
# Cut into 1/2-inch slices.
# Melt butter in a frying pan, and fry until golden brown and heated all the way through.

Source: Telesco, pg 133
