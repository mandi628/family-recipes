# Ramen Pad Thai

Total Time: 10 min  

Serves: 1  


### Ingredients
* 1 pkg Ramen Noodles (you won't use the seasoning packet)
* 2T C creamy peanut butter
* 1-1/2 tsp soy sauce
* 1-1/2 tsp brown sugar
* 1-1/2 tsp lime juice
* 3/4 tsp chili garlic sauce or Siracha
* 1 t toasted sesame oil
* 1 t vegetable oil
* 1 large eggs, well beaten
* 1 scallions or green onions, diced
* crushed peanuts, for garnish
* lime wedges, for serving

**Add-ins**

* fresh bean sprouts
* grated carrot
* 6 oz. pan-fried tofu, marinated in soy sauce

# In a large saucepan, cook the Ramen noodles according to the package directions, 2-3 minutes until just tender.
# Drain most of the water, reserving 2 Tablespoons.
# In a medium bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, chili sauce, sesame oil, and reserved Ramen water. Set aside.
# Place saucepan back on the heat over medium heat and add a bit of vegetable oil. When hot, add the eggs and scramble until cooked through.
# Remove pan from the heat and add the cooked noodles and sauce. Toss to coat.
# Serve immediately, topped with green onions, crushed peanuts, and lime wedges.

### Notes
* You can increase the spice with more chili sauce or crushed red pepper flakes.
* Add or switch the protein by stirring in some cooked chicken, port, or shrimp at the end.
