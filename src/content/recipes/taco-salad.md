# Taco Salad

Yields: 8 svgs

## Ingredients
* 1 lb. ground beef, or equivalent TVP
* 1 onion, diced
* 1 green pepper, diced
* 1 pkg. [taco seasoning](Taco Seasoning.html)
* 1 lb. box tri-color rotini pasta (screws), or any preferred shape
* 1-2 heads Romaine lettuce, chopping
* 1-2 large tomoatoes, diced or 1 pkg grape tomatoes, halved
* 2-4 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
* 1 cup Catalina style salad dressing

### Optional add-ins
* 1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
* 1 can whole-kernel corn, drained
* replace beef with shredded chicken

## Directions
# Brown ground beef in a pan, and add onions and green peppers until cooked.
#* If using TVP, prepare separately and set aside. Saute onions and green peppers in a pan.
# Following package directions, add taco seasoning to the pan and simmer until thickened.
#* If using TVP, add prepared TVP to onions & peppers, and add taco seasoning as above.
# Remove meat mixture from pan and refrigerate to cool.
# Following package directions, cook noodles. When done, drain and set aside.
# Combine cooled noodles with meat mixture. Stir in lettuce, tomatoes, cheddar cheese, and dressing.
# Serve topped with crumbled Doritos - Nacho flavor.
