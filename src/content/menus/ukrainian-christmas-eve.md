It is traditional to serve 12 meatless dishes on Christmas Eve. (Stechyshyn, 17) The twelve dishes represent the twelve disciples. The meatless element, I believe, it to represent Jesus sharing the fish and loaves to the masses at his Sermon on the Mount.
